# Welcome to Precisive Books LLC

Precisive Books LLC provides affordable mobile and remote notary and bookkeeping services to individuals and small businesses to save them money and protect them against costly legal disputes in the future.

## Our Services

### Mobile Notary Services
We offer mobile notary sessions for customers to save them time and to provide them access to notarizations that are not recognized through an electronic notary stamp.

[Learn More About Mobile Notary Services →](/mobile-notary-services/)

### Remote Online Notary (RON)
We offer remote notarization services for customers all across the U.S. Once you book a remote notarization session, you will go through simple steps to ensure a seamless notarization experience.

[Learn More About Remote Notary Services →](/remote-notary-services/)

### Bookkeeping Services
Professional bookkeeping services for small businesses and independent contractors at affordable rates. We help you maintain accurate financial records and protect against costly legal disputes.
