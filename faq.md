### How does Remote Online Notarization work?

1. Request availability with your document type and timeline
2. Join a secure video session at your scheduled time
3. Verify your identity with valid government-issued ID
4. Review and sign documents electronically
5. Receive notarized documents immediately

### What documents can be notarized remotely?

Power of Attorney documents, affidavits, loan documents, real estate documents, and various legal documents. Contact us to confirm if your specific document type can be notarized remotely.

### What do I need for a remote notarization?

- Valid government-issued photo ID (driver's license, passport)
- Device with camera and microphone (smartphone, tablet, or computer)
- Stable internet connection
- Documents in digital format (PDF preferred)

### Is remote notarization secure?

Yes. We use secure audio-visual technology, identity verification, and encrypted document transmission. Every execution is Virginia statute-compliant.
