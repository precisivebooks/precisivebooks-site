# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## Remote Online Notary (RON) Questions

### What is a Remote Online Notary (RON)?

A Remote Online Notary is a commissioned notary public authorized to notarize documents online using secure audio-visual technology. This means you can have documents notarized from anywhere using your smartphone, tablet, or computer.

### How does Remote Online Notarization work?

1. Schedule your remote notarization session
2. Join a secure video call at your scheduled time
3. Verify your identity with valid ID
4. Review and sign your documents electronically
5. Receive your notarized documents immediately

### What documents can be notarized remotely?

Many document types can be notarized remotely, including Power of Attorney documents, affidavits, loan documents, real estate documents, and various legal documents. Contact us to confirm if your specific document type can be notarized remotely.

### What do I need for a remote notarization?

- Valid government-issued photo ID (driver's license, passport, etc.)
- Device with camera and microphone (smartphone, tablet, or computer)
- Stable internet connection
- Documents in digital format (PDF preferred)

### Is remote notarization secure?

Yes, remote notarization uses secure audio-visual technology, identity verification processes, and encrypted document transmission to ensure the security and validity of your notarized documents.
