# Mobile Notary Services in Northern Virginia

Precisive Books LLC is a Virginia-commissioned mobile notary based in Chantilly. I travel to your home, office, care facility, or closing table on weekday evenings and weekends so you don't have to take time off work or wait for the courthouse to reopen.

## Documents I Notarize

- **Powers of attorney** — financial, general, durable, and limited
- **Healthcare directives** — living wills, advance medical directives, HIPAA releases
- **Affidavits and sworn statements**
- **Vehicle documents** — bills of sale, title transfers, foreign vehicle paperwork
- **Trust documents** — revocable, irrevocable, and amendments
- **Real estate documents** — deeds, refinance packages, loan signings
- **Personal legal forms** — school, medical, travel, immigration, parental consent

If you're unsure whether your document needs a notary—or whether I can handle it—[send me a quick message](/contact/) and I'll confirm before you book.

## Pricing

## Why a Mobile Notary?

### Convenience
You don't take time off work. You don't drive to a bank that might not have a notary on duty. You don't fight courthouse parking. I show up where you are.

### Reliability
Virginia-commissioned, insured, and background-screened. I follow Virginia Secretary of the Commonwealth guidelines on every notarization.

### Speed
Most appointments take 15–30 minutes. Multiple documents? Bring them all to one visit and save on travel.

## How It Works

1. **Contact me** — Call (571) 306-3551, email matthew@precisivebooks.com, or use the [contact form](/contact/). Include the date, time, location, and a brief description of the documents.
2. **Confirm your appointment** — I'll confirm availability, total cost, and any prep instructions (what to bring, what not to sign in advance).
3. **I come to you** — At the scheduled time, at your home, office, or chosen location anywhere in the service area.
4. **Notarization** — I verify your ID, witness the signature, apply the seal, and complete the journal entry.
5. **Done** — Your documents leave with you, fully notarized.

## What to Bring

- A valid, unexpired government-issued photo ID (driver's license, passport, state ID, or military ID)
- The **unsigned** documents (do not sign before I arrive)
- Any required witnesses (some documents need one or two—I'll let you know in advance)
- Payment: cash, check, or card
